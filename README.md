# Insta Liner

Part of the Fridge Combo project. Keep your Instagram captions perfectly formatted with invisible line breaks.

## 1 Tool / 1 Action

Just paste your draft. **Insta Liner** inserts specialized invisible characters into your empty lines, ensuring that Instagram's formatting engine doesn't "clump" your text together. No more using dots (.) or symbols to keep a clean space.

## Philosophy
* **1 Tool, 1 Action.**
* **No Login / No Ads / No Tracking**
* **Browser-only Privacy** (Works on low-spec devices)

## How to Use
1. Paste your draft with empty lines.
2. Click **Convert & Copy**.
3. Paste it directly into your Instagram caption.
